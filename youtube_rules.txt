YOUTUBE ADVERTISER-FRIENDLY GUIDELINES (SHORTS FOCUS)

1. Profanity & Vulgarity:
- Banned in the first 7 seconds: All heavy profanity (f-word, etc.). Triggers immediate demonetization.
- Allowed after 7 seconds: Moderate use of profanity, provided it is not the focal point of the video.
- Never allowed: Slurs, hate speech, or derogatory terms directed at protected groups.

2. Violence & Shocking Content:
- Not allowed: Graphic violence, real-world injury, or highly realistic fake violence.
- Allowed: Mild, comedic, or clearly animated/video game violence (provided it is not excessively gory).

3. Sensitive Events & Controversies:
- High Risk: Mentioning breaking real-world tragedies, active conflicts, or massive brand scandals without an educational/journalistic tone.

4. Dangerous Acts (Shorts Specific):
- Not allowed: Challenges or pranks that carry an imminent risk of physical injury or emotional trauma.
